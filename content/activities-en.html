Cats Unlimited

A very exciting leopard and cheetah drive, which takes approximately 45 to 50 minutes. Here you can take excellent photos as the distance to the cats will be only 10 to 15 meters. Departure time 14.00 (april until september); 15.30 (september until april).

Horse riding

Start your trip through Namibia with a few days of riding here on our farm. An experience you will not forget soon and more intense then regular game viewing. We now also have a special half-board package for 3 days with a local guide.
